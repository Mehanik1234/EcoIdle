About The Project

EcoIdle is a calm city builder about growth, economy, and ecology. Expand markets, launch industry, and keep pollution in check to protect your citizens.

Use the tech tree to plan long-term progress and see requirements before you commit.

Quick Tips

  • Start with markets to create steady income.
  • Balance pollution with farms and cleanup facilities.
  • Watch taxes: large fines slow down progress.
  • Plan your tech branches early, some nodes need big population.

Ecology Risks

High pollution brings fines, population loss, and random crises. The best cities grow when profit and clean air stay balanced.

Clean tech is slower for income, but keeps your citizens safe and stable.

Roadmap

  • More random events and city challenges.
  • Expanded technology tree with extra branches.
  • Visual milestones and new UI feedback.
  • More ways to grow without destroying the environment.
